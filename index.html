Privacy Policy

Flipstar does not collect, store, transmit, or share personal or sensitive user data. We do not require you to create an account, log in, or submit any information such as name, email, phone number, or location.

Flipstar does not collect device identifiers, advertising IDs, crash logs, analytics, usage events, or location data. We do not use cookies or tracking technologies.

Flipstar may store game-related data locally on your device, such as progress, settings, or preferences, using on-device storage. This data:

Flipstar does not use third-party services, including but not limited to:

Flipstar does not require an internet connection to function and does not access online resources. The app is designed to run fully offline.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share any personal or sensitive data with third parties because we do not collect it. We do not sell, rent, or trade user data.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Any locally stored game data remains on your device. If you uninstall the app, local app data may be removed by your device and could be lost permanently. We do not have the ability to recover it because it is not stored on our servers.

7. Security

Since Flipstar does not collect or transmit data, the risk of server-side data breaches is minimized. However, you are responsible for maintaining the security of your device.

Flipstar is safe for users of all ages and does not collect personal information from anyone, including children. Because no data is collected, we do not process children’s personal data. If you have any questions, please contact us using the email below.
