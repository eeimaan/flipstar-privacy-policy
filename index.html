Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

Flipstar does not collect, store, transmit, or share personal or sensitive user data. We do not require you to create an account, log in, or submit information such as name, email, phone number, or location.

1.1 Information You Provide

Flipstar does not ask you to provide personal information. Gameplay interactions remain within the app on your device.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

Flipstar does not collect device identifiers, advertising IDs, crash logs, usage analytics, location data, or other tracking information. We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies.

2. Local Device Storage

Flipstar may store game-related data locally on your device (for example: progress, settings, or preferences) using on-device storage. This data:

  • stays only on your device,
  • is not transmitted to us or any server,
  • is not accessible by us.

3. Third-Party Services

Flipstar does not use third-party services for data collection, including but not limited to:

  • Advertisements / ad networks
  • Analytics services (e.g., Firebase Analytics)
  • Crash reporting SDKs
  • Cloud storage / online backup
  • Social media integrations
  • Tracking technologies

4. Internet Usage & External Links

Flipstar does not require an internet connection for gameplay. However, if you choose to open external links (such as this Privacy Policy page, support email, or other help links), the app may launch your device’s system browser or email app.

We do not collect, track, or transmit any personal data when you open external links. Any data handling on external websites is governed by the respective website’s own privacy policy.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share personal or sensitive data with third parties because we do not collect it. We do not sell, rent, or trade user data.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Any locally stored game data remains on your device. If you uninstall the app, local data may be removed by your device and could be lost permanently. We cannot recover it because it is not stored on our servers.

7. Security

Since Flipstar does not collect or transmit data, server-side data breach risk is minimized. You are responsible for the security of your device.

8. Children’s Privacy

Flipstar is safe for users of all ages and does not collect personal information from anyone, including children. Because no data is collected, we do not process children’s personal data.

9. International Data Transfers

We do not transfer data internationally because Flipstar does not transmit data to us or any servers.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
